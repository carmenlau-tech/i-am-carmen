001 : International School at ZhengCheng

The studio calls for a design of an international school at ZhengCheng, China. The proposed development consists of a kindergarten block, a primary school block, and a dormitory block. Physcial models are hand-made first to explore the forms and space of the buildings.

Studio: TCA || Role: Architectural Designer || Year: 2021-2022
