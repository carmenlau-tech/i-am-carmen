003 : Revitalization of Bishop Hill Underground Cistern

Demolition of an underground reservoir that is believed to have been completed in 1904 was suspended after online images sparked public calls to preserve it as a heritage site. A design proposal for the revitalization is made to showcase how heritage site could be re-examined; promote co-existence of building and nature; and celebrate the depth and characteristics of Hong Kong’s infrastructure while contributing to the general public.

Year: 2021
